Donut Clicker

Fred the Baker loves two things in this world, making donuts and playing cookie clicker. That's why Fred tasked WCCI to put their top students to the test to combine his two loves. Cookie clicker is a game where you click on a cookie to get cookies. Collect enough cookies and you can purchase auto-clickers to click for you! In Fred's mind, what's better than clicking a cookie? You guessed it! Clicking a donut! So go ahead and click on that delicious, strawberry frosted, sprinkle donut! The more donuts you have, the bigger the discount you can when you purchase your own strawberry sprinkle donut from Fred, himself! Happy clicking!
